Prime New York Strip

Delicious Foods proudly serves aged USDA Prime beef, corn-fed, hand-selected and aged a minimum of 35 days. USDA Prime corn-fed beef has abundant marbling that makes the beef more tender, juicy and flavorful. We are proud to offer you these superb steaks. All prime steaks are seasoned with olive oil & kosher salt.
